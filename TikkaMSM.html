Tikka Masala Spice Mix

  • 5 mins
  • Homemade staple
  • Make-ahead
  • Pantry-ready

Tikka masala is a blend of aromatic and warm spices. The sweet paprika powder and chili give it a signature red colour. It's great to use in preparing Indian-inspired dishes like our Quick Tofu Tikka Masala recipe.

  • Total
  • 5 mins
  • Prep
  • 5 mins

Ingredients

  • 2 Tbsp (11 g) ground coriander
  • 2 Tbsp (14 g) paprika powder
  • 1 Tbsp (6 g) ground cumin
  • 1 tsp (1 g) dried fenugreek leaves
  • 1 tsp (3 g) chili powder
  • 1 tsp (2 g) ground ginger
  • 1 tsp (3 g) ground turmeric
  • ½ tsp ground nutmeg
  • ½ tsp ground cinnamon
  • ¼ tsp ground cardamom
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper

Directions

  1. Mix all the spices together in a small jar. Enjoy!
