Strawberry Milk Bubble Tea

  • 25 mins
  • Drink

The vibrant strawberry purée adds a delicious juicy burst of flavour to the creamy, sweet milk. The chewy pearls add a wonderful texture and makes this a filling summer drink.

  • Total
  • 25 mins
  • Prep
  • 10 mins
  • Cook
  • 15 min

Ingredients

  • 1½ cups (225 g) frozen strawberries
  • 1 Tbsp (14 g) granulated sugar
  • ½ cup (76 g) quick-cooking tapioca pearls
  • 2 Tbsp (30 mL) agave syrup
  • ⅔ cup (160 mL) unsweetened oat milk†
  • 2 Tbsp (30 mL) vegan whipping cream (optional)
  • 1 tsp (5 mL) pure vanilla extract
  • 2 cups (222 g) ice cubes

Optional toppings

  • fresh Strawberry

Directions

  1. To a saucepan, add the frozen strawberries and sugar. Cook on medium heat until the strawberries have thawed. Use the back of a spoon to lightly mash the strawberries.
  2. Remove the pan from the stove and use an immersion blender or standing blender to purée the strawberries. Set aside to cool.
  3. Cook the tapioca pearls according to the package instructions. For us, this took 5 minutes.
  4. Drain the tapioca pearls, and rinse under cold water to stop the cooking process. Add the pearls to a bowl and mix in the agave syrup.
  5. In a pitcher, combine the oat milk, whipping cream, and vanilla.
  6. Divide the tapioca pearls between your glasses, and spoon some of the strawberry purée over the pearls.
  7. Top with ice, followed by the vanilla cream milk. Finish with some fresh strawberries if desired. Enjoy!
